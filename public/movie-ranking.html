Top Movies of All Time According to Me

What I think are the best 3 movies

Lady and the Tramp

Being a hopeless romantic, I've always loved a good love story. There's just been something about it that's always stuck with me.

Lion King 2: Simba's Pride

Similar to the last entry, I love a good love story. Plus the soundtrack is 10/10, it's very underrated compared to the success of the first film. That and I think I watched it every day one summer in middle school.

Guillermo del Toro's Pinocchio

Now this is a new one for me. For the longest time I would've filled this slot with Thor: The Dark World or maybe with Black Panther. Those movies are pretty renowned already, though, so I figured I'd highlight something that didn't get much hooplah. I've never been big on the Pinocchio story but this particular telling really rips your heart out and makes you think.
